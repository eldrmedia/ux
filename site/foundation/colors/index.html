Colors

Color Scheme

Used for elements that must reflect Roll20's brand. With the exception of the light and dark shades, each color has a contrast ratio well within the WCAG 2 Level AA requirement. Lighter and dark shades are to be used as background colors with a contrasting text color.

Ensure whatever color you choose should have a contrast ratio of 4.5:1 from it's background color.

Gray Colors

Grey colors are used for text, backgrounds, lines and borders. The grays from 100 to 300 are used for backgrounds, while the ones from 400 to 600 are used for icons.

Reversible Grays

Each light gray color has a complementary dark grey color. This helps keeping the colors well balanced and also makes it easier to implement light/dark themes. Good example of this is the Marvel editor, which is implemented in the dark theme.

Light theme

This subtle paragraph text color works equally well on a light or dark background.

.bg-gray-200

.bg-gray-400

Dark theme

This subtle paragraph text color works equally well on a light or dark background.

.bg-gray-900

.bg-gray-800
